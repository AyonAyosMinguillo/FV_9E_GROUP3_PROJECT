Our History


Since 1966, O.B Montessori was founded by Dr. Preciosa Soliven and Oscar Arellano. Since then, we have dedicated our efforts in providing quality Montessori education to the youth of the Philippines. Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center started with humble beginnings, starting off in Pasay City as a preschool in 1971. A year later, OBMC was now a full on school with a new building in Sta. Ana. OBMC then branched out to other places of the Philippines, spreading the Montessorian teaching style to Angeles City, Greenhills, Las Pinas, and finally Fairview.
